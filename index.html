About me:

Hi, I’m Amaan—an aspiring Mathematics and Computing undergraduate who enjoys approaching problems from first principles and uncovering their underlying mathematical structure.

My interests lie at the intersection of mathematics, machine learning, data science, and scientific computing, particularly in understanding how mathematical ideas manifest in modern AI and statistical models.

I primarily work with C and Python, frequently using NumPy, Matplotlib, and Manim for numerical experiments and visualizations, along with LaTeX for clearly communicating mathematical ideas.

A firm proponent of open-source philosophy, I find great value in the collaborative development of transparent and accessible software. I am always eager to engage in rigorous, exploratory projects and welcome the opportunity to contribute to collective solutions alongside fellow developers and researchers. Should you wish to discuss a potential collaboration or share insights on a particular problem, I invite you to reach out.
